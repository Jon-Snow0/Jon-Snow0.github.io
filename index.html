This is the custom gaming PC that I parted out and built myself when I wanted to get into PC gaming.
Key Specs: Intel i7 4790k, EVGA GTX 980 Superclocked, 16GB HyperX Fury DDR3 1633MHz RAM, Samsung 840 Evo 250 GB SSD, WD Blue 1TB HDD, Corsair H100i AIO Water Cooler.

A Client's Gaming PC (2016)

I built this gaming PC for a client who wanted to play the latest AAA titles without compromising.
Key Specs: Intel i7 6700k, EVGA GTX 1080 Superclocked, 16GB HyperX Fury DDR3 1633MHz RAM, Samsung 850 Evo 250 GB SSD, WD Blue 1TB HDD, Corsair H100i GTX AIO Water Cooler.
